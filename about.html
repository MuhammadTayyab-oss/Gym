Our Story

We have fought battles over different planets. i.e Planet Namek, Earth. Not only planets but also in different universes and against fighters of all 12 universes and we are the winners of tournament of power. What we do

We help people to learn martial arts and also how to train. We train them to fight against the evil monsters and also people who are against humanity and cause destruction or hurdles to humans.
